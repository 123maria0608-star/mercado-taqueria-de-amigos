Our Story

Don't Judge a Taco
by its Gas Station

What looks like an ordinary stop on Pescadero Creek Road is actually one of the best kept secrets on the San Mateo coast. Step inside and you'll find made-to-order tacos, burritos, and a salsa bar that'll make you rethink everything you know about "gas station food."

From crispy fish tacos loaded with fresh cabbage slaw, to slow-braised carnitas that melt in your mouth — every item is made with care, flavor, and a whole lot of love. The salsa bar alone is worth stopping for.
